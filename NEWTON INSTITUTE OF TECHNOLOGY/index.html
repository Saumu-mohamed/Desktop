  • History and long-term goals of NIT

    • History and Long-Term Goals

    Founded with the vision of bridging the gap between education and industry, Newton Institute of Technology (NIT) started as a small technical training college and has evolved into a full-fledged institution offering programs in ICT, Plant Operations, Beauty Therapy, Music, and Driving.

    Admissions

      Step-by-step application guide

      1. Visit our official website or admissions office.
      2. Fill out the online application form.
      3. Upload required documents (ID, academic transcripts, certificates).
      4. Pay the application fee through the provided options.
      5. Await communication regarding interview or entrance requirements (if applicable).
      6. Receive admission letter and proceed with registration and fee payment.

      Fee Structure

      Fees vary by course. Below is a sample breakdown:

      • Full Stack Development: KES 90,000 full course
      • Plant Operator Training: KES 80,000 full course
      • Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy: KES 60,000 full course
      • Basic computer skills: Starting from KES 6,000

      Flexible payment are available. Contact the admissions office for full details.

      Calendar of Intake Periods

      • January Intake: Applications open in November
      • May Intake: Applications open in March
      • September Intake: Applications open in July

      Early application is advised due to limited slots per program.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Answers to common queries

    Q: How do I apply to NIT?
    A: You can apply online through our website or visit our admissions office for guidance.
    Q: What are the intake periods?
    A: We have three intakes annually: January, May, and September.
    Q: What makes NIT different?
    A: NIT emphasizes hands-on, industry-relevant training, supportive mentorship, and real-world skill development.
    Q: Are the courses accredited?
    A: Yes, all our programs are accredited by relevant government and industry bodies.
